promoting good nutrition and susutainable food systems

What is SDG 2?

SDG 2 aims to end hunger, achieve food security, improve nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture worldwide. It focuses on giving everyone access to safe, nutritious, and sufficient food all year round.

Whay SDG 2 is Important

Hunger affects millions of people worldwide. Poor nutrition leads to disease and slow development. Sustainable farming ensures long-term food security.

Key Targets of SDG 2

End all forms of hunger and malnutrition. Double agricultural productivity. Promote sustainable food production systems. Maintain genetic diversity of crops and animals.
